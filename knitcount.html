Simple, intuitive design and animation
makes for the cutest and easiest to use row counter available.

Unlimited Projects

Add as many projects as your heart desires and easily go back to them at any time. Quickly toggle between your Finished Objects and your Work In Progress. You can add notes and pictures too!

Unlimited Counters

You can have as many or as little counters per project as you want. Quickly pick from preset counters for row, repeat, increase and decrease, or create your own custom counter.

Fun colors

8 hand-picked colors allow for the app to be customized to your unique style, while still being easy to read and not distracting.

Fun sounds

Tapping counters and finishing projects produce fun sounds. You can even hear a cat's meow on every tap, or a frog go "rippit" when you undo a row. Not your thing? Disable the sounds in the settings.
